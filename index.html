About Me

Featured Pexels Photographer | 9th Annual International 35 AWARDS Winner

Visual Storytelling

I am an award-winning travel photographer and filmmaker specializing in landscape and cultural photography. My work captures the raw beauty of Nepal—from the majestic Himalayas to intimate cultural moments—through a lens that honors both nature and humanity.

Photography Practice

My photography is defined by a deep connection to place and people. I work primarily in landscape, nature, and ethnographic storytelling, documenting diverse communities and ecosystems across Nepal. Each image is a meditation on light, composition, and the stories embedded in everyday moments.

Creative Direction

As Vice President and Media Manager at Bhaktapur Junior Jaycees, I led visual projects that bridged creative excellence with community impact. My work combines technical precision with artistic vision to create compelling narratives through photography and film.

My Philosophy

"Every photograph is a conversation between the lens and the world. I capture moments that reveal the soul of place and the essence of culture."
"Photography is my language. Through it, I translate the beauty I witness into images that inspire, move, and connect people across boundaries."
